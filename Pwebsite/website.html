The experience that has shaped who I am today and driven me to be as motivated as I am to pursue becoming a computer engineer was not a single event, but rather the culmination of my life experience. I was born in the country-side of Nepal with no access to computers, televisions, electricity, phones, or other common infrastructure. The resources were limited, but I learned enough about technology and its potential global impact to keep me dreaming about the possibility of someday obtaining an education that would allow me to work in an information technology role.
